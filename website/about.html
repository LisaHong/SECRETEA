Nice to matcha—we’re SECRETEA!

Welcome to our website! We’re glad you found us. If you enjoy boba or bubble tea (whichever you prefer to call it) as much as we do, then you’re in the right place. We can never get tea-nough of it!

SECRETEA began as a project for a Foundations of Software Engineering class at California State University, Fullerton by Lisa Hong, a huge boba connoisseur. The inspiration behind the creation of this website was purely driven by her love for boba. Sometimes you don’t want to order something from the menu, but, you don’t know if there’s a secret menu for some of these boba shops. Instead of searching through several sketchy websites, why not compile it all in one and allow users to give their review on the drinks?

Sounds like it was a tea-rrific idea, doesn’t it? What are you waiting for? Let’s get this par-tea started!
